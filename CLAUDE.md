# HomesteadxCreate Modpack
 
## Vision

A cozy, rewarding multiplayer Minecraft 1.21.1 (NeoForge) modpack that marries the warm, immersive atmosphere of the Homestead modpack with a heavy steam/diesel-punk aesthetic powered by the Create mod ecosystem.

The core idea: take everything that makes Homestead _feel_ good — the decoration depth, personalization, ambient detail, sense of discovery, and satisfying progression — and replace the manual homestead activities (farming by hand, simple crafting loops, etc.) with Create-driven automation and engineering. You're not a farmer in a cottage. You're an engineer in a cozy workshop, building machines that do the farming/mining for you while the world feels alive and beautiful around you.

## Tone & Aesthetic

- **Cozy** — warm lighting, ambient sounds, livable spaces, rewarding moment-to-moment play
 - **Steam/diesel-punk** — brass, iron, smoke, gears, contraptions, power networks
- **Adventure** — exploration, discovery, light RPG elements
- **NOT homesteady** — no emphasis on manual farming, animal husbandry loops, or cottage-core simplicity as the end goal. Those systems may exist as _inputs_ to automation, not as the point.

## Design Principles
1. **Progression through engineering** — the player advances by building better machines, not grinding manual tasks
2. **Every space should feel livable** — strong decoration and ambience mods ensure builds feel cozy, not industrial-ugly
3. **Exploration rewards engineering** — the world should give you reasons to fly your aeronautic contraptions and build diesel-powered expeditions
4. **Stability over content** — fewer, well-tested mods beat a bloated kitchen sink. Pin exact versions.
5. **Multiplayer-first** — balanced and tested around multiplayer play. The server should be rock solid and provide pathways for people to work together or solo to progress in the world.
