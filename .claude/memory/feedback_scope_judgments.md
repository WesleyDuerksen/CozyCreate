Don't predict how many mods will end up in the final list. The user removed a statement saying "Phase 3 will cut this significantly, goal is 10–20 not all 87."

**Why:** We don't know yet. It could be all 87 or it could be far fewer. Premature anchoring biases the evaluation before it happens.

**How to apply:** In research phases, present candidates neutrally. Let the filter criteria do the work. Don't pre-summarize outcomes.

The core filter lens for CozyCreate is: "cozy container around automation and exploration." That's the frame — not minimalism, not maximalism.
