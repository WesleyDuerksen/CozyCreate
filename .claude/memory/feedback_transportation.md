All transportation must be infrastructure the players build — no teleportation mods or flying craft outside Create: Aeronautics.

**Why:** The modpack's identity is engineering-driven progression. A single block that warps you anywhere undermines the point of building a rail network or airship. Transport mods should reward players who invest in infrastructure, not shortcut it.

**How to apply:**
- Drop Waystones, Via Romana, Immersive Aircraft, and any similar mods
- The only aerial travel is Create: Aeronautics
- Ground travel is Create: Steam 'n' Rails (trains) and horses/vanilla
- If someone suggests a travel/teleport mod, decline and suggest a Create-compatible equivalent instead
