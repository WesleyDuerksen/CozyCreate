## Column C — In Shortlist, Not in AoCA (Informational)

AoCA made different choices here. Worth noting to understand where we diverge.

### Create (our additions, not in AoCA)
| Mod | Note |
|-----|------|
| Create: Steam 'n' Rails | AoCA uses Train Parts + Railways Navigator instead. Different philosophy — more modular. |
| Create: Track Map | AoCA uses Railways Navigator's built-in map instead |

### Sound & Atmosphere (our stack, absent from AoCA)
| Mod | Note |
|-----|------|
| AmbientSounds | AoCA uses Biome Music instead — music tracks vs. ambient soundscapes |
| Sound Physics Remastered | Completely absent from AoCA — they don't model reverb/attenuation |
| LambDynamicLights | AoCA uses Sodium Dynamic Lights (NeoForge native) |
| Particle Rain | Absent — AoCA doesn't make weather atmospheric |
| Subtle Effects | Absent |

### Decoration (our cozy layer, absent from AoCA)
Chipped, Decorative Lamps, Diagonal Walls/Windows, Double Doors, Dusty Decorations, Immersive Furniture, Macaw's series, Mo Glass, Serene Shrubbery, Simple Hats, Sooty Chimneys, Armor Statues, Dark Paintings, Camerapture, Antique Atlas 4, Beautify!

**Why it matters:** AoCA's Create decoration (Design n' Decor, Vibrant Vaults, Industrialized Architecture) replaces where we use generic decoration mods. Their spaces look functional/industrial. Ours need to look cozy. We keep both layers.

### World Gen (entirely absent from AoCA)
Tectonic, Nature's Spirit, Geophilic, Regions Unexplored, Oh The Biomes We've Gone, Incendium, Eldritch End, Serene Seasons, Autumnity, Environmental, Ecologics, Alex's Mobs, Friends & Foes, Upgrade Aquatic, Towns and Towers, Tidal Towns, Dungeons & Taverns, Repurposed Structures, Valhelsia Structures, YUNG's Better series, Explorer's/Nature's Compass, Traveller's Titles, Lootr

**Why it matters:** AoCA treats the vanilla world as a backdrop for Create. We treat the world as something worth flying over and exploring. Our entire World Gen section is a CozyCreate-specific addition that AoCA doesn't compete with.

### Food Ecosystem (our cozy layer, absent from AoCA)
Let's Do Vinery, HerbalBrews, Beachparty, Meadow, WilderNature — Ocean's/End's/Nature's Delight, Chef's Delight, Crate Delight, Comforts, Duckling, Realistic Bees, RightClickHarvest, Crops Love Rain, Universal Bone Meal, Flower Tweaks

**Why it matters:** AoCA replaced most of this with Create food mods. CozyCreate uses Create to automate the supply while keeping the player-facing cooking loop social and tactile.

### Storage (our selection vs. AoCA's absence)
Sophisticated Backpacks/Storage, Storage Drawers, Tom's Simple Storage, Trash Cans, TrashSlot, Carry On — all absent from AoCA. They use no dedicated storage system beyond vanilla chests and FTB.

### Multiplayer Infrastructure (our additions)
Simple Voice Chat, Chunky — absent from AoCA.

### Performance (our extras)
Noisium, Entity Culling, Debugify — absent from AoCA.

### Scripting
KubeJS, CraftTweaker — absent from AoCA. They use custom data pack mods instead.

### Death Handling
Universal Graves (our choice) vs. Corpse (AoCA's choice) — different mods doing the same job.
